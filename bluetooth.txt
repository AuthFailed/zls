In order to pair bluetooth headsets run
	bluetoothctl

Run, in order
	1. power on
	2. agent on
	3. default-agent
	4. scan on

When your device appears run
	scan off
then copy the MAC Address of the chosen device

Run
	1. pair ${your_device_mac_address}
	2. connect ${your_device_mac_address}

If you get org.bluez.Error.Failed quit and kill pulseaudio with
	pulseaudio -k
then retry

In order to auto-connect your device add the following to /etc/deafult.pa
	# automatically switch to newly-connected devices
	load-module module-switch-on-connect

then, in bluetoothctl
	trust ${your_device_mac_address}
